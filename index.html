Welcome to the 2019 Design in Spaces Conference!

Together we can explore how the designs around us interact with our daily lives. Through this website, you can submit a proposal for either a session or a proposal for consideration within a proposal. With many categories to choose from, you can find the perfect place for your proposal. We will be accepting applications for the 2019 Design in Spaces until July 31, 2019. Make sure your application is submitted in a timely manner, so our judges have time to consider your proposal.

Why should I apply?

The Design in Spaces Conference was developed to give a voice to artist, architects, and other media designers. We focus on how the design around us changes the way we interact with our environement. By applying to the Design in Spaces Conference, you are given the chance to share your proposal with the over 5,000 people that attend our conference every year.

2019 Design in Spaces Conference Scholarship

This year we are offering a scholarship to applicants for the Design in Spaces Conference. This years sponsor is OpenWater, a software platform that helps grow your awards, fellowships, grants, and more! The deadline for applying to the Design in Spaces Scholarship is May 1, 2019. The following is included in the scholarship:
  • Roundtrip Airfare to/from the conference.
  • $2,500 Stipend for expenses during the conference.
  • Hotel and dinners for all three days.
  • Personal mentor. Your personal mentor will guide you through the conference to ensure you get the most out of attendence.
Requirements:
  • Must be a first time attendee.
  • Be at least 18 years old.
  • Must have a proposal submitted in the Design in Spaces Conference.
